Food Culture of Uttarakhand

Aaloo Ke Gutke

Aaloo Ke Gutke is a popular dish in Uttarakhand made with boiled potatoes saut√©ed with spices. It is often served with puri or roti and is a favorite among locals and tourists alike.

Kafuli

Kafuli is a traditional dish made with spinach and fenugreek leaves cooked in a thick gravy. It is a nutritious and flavorful dish that is best enjoyed with steamed rice.

Bhang Ki Chutney

Bhang Ki Chutney is a unique chutney made from roasted hemp seeds, cumin, and other spices. It is a popular accompaniment to various dishes in Uttarakhand and adds a burst of flavor to any meal.

Chainsoo

Chainsoo is a traditional dish made from black gram dal (urad dal) that is roasted and ground into a coarse powder. It is then cooked with spices to create a rich and flavorful curry.

Baadi

Baadi is a simple yet nutritious dish made from buckwheat flour. It is cooked with water and served with ghee and a side of green leafy vegetables or dal.

Jhangora Ki Kheer

Jhangora Ki Kheer is a traditional dessert made from barnyard millet (jhangora) cooked in milk and flavored with cardamom and saffron. It is a popular sweet dish in Uttarakhand, especially during festivals.

Arsa

Arsa is a traditional sweet made from rice flour, jaggery, and sesame seeds. It is deep-fried to create a crispy and delicious treat that is enjoyed during festivals and special occasions.

Singori

Singori is a unique sweet dish made from khoya (reduced milk) wrapped in a leaf. It is a specialty of the Kumaon region and is known for its distinctive flavor and presentation.
