Arunachal Pradesh: Land of the Dawn-Lit Mountains

Tawang Monastery

The largest monastery in India and second largest in the world, showcasing the rich Buddhist heritage of Arunachal Pradesh.

Tribal Heritage

Home to 26 major tribes and over 100 sub-tribes, each with their unique customs, languages, and traditional practices.

Festivals

Celebrations like Losar, Nyokum, and Solung showcase the diverse cultural traditions of different tribes throughout the year.
