Manipur: The Jewel of India

Ras Leela

The classical Manipuri dance form, particularly the Ras Leela, is one of the most graceful classical dance forms of India, characterized by gentle and lyrical movements.

Loktak Lake

The largest freshwater lake in Northeast India, famous for its unique floating islands called phumdis and the only floating national park in the world.

Traditional Cuisine

Manipuri cuisine is characterized by its use of rice, fish, and various local herbs and vegetables. The traditional black rice and Eromba are local delicacies.
