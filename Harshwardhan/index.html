North East India is home to over 220 ethnic groups and an equal number of dialects.The hills and valleys of the region echo with fascinating folklore passed down through generations.

Each state celebrates numerous festivals throughout the year, showcasing their unique traditions, dance forms, and culinary delights.

Natural Beauty

From the mighty Brahmaputra to the living root bridges of Meghalaya, the region is blessed with incredible natural wonders.
