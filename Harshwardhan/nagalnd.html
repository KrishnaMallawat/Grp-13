Nagaland: Land of Festivals

Hornbill Festival

Known as the 'Festival of Festivals', the Hornbill Festival showcases the rich cultural heritage and traditions of all Naga tribes.

Traditional Attire

Each Naga tribe has its distinct traditional attire, featuring colorful shawls and beaded jewelry that signify social status and achievements.

Naga Cuisine

Known for its unique flavors and use of ghost peppers, Naga cuisine includes dishes like Smoked Pork and Axone (fermented soybean).
