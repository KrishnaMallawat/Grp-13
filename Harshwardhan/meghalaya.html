Meghalaya: Abode of Clouds

Living Root Bridges

The famous living root bridges of Meghalaya are hand-made from the aerial roots of rubber trees by the Khasi and Jaintia peoples.

Khasi Culture

The Khasi people follow a matrilineal system where lineage and inheritance are traced through the mother's clan.

Cherrapunji

Once known as the wettest place on Earth, Cherrapunji showcases the unique relationship between the people and their environment.
