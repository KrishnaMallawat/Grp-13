Mizoram: Land of the Hill People

Cheraw Dance

The bamboo dance is Mizoram's most famous cultural dance, requiring great skill and coordination between dancers.

Chapchar Kut

A spring festival celebrated after the completion of jhum cultivation, featuring traditional dances, music, and games.

Traditional Cuisine

Mizo cuisine is known for its simplicity and use of local ingredients, with dishes like Bai and Misa Mach Poora being local favorites.
